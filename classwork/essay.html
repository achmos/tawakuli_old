What You'll Wish You'd Known

The essay that this response is written for is contained here.

  This is a very mind opening essay. Its basically a key to doing better in high school and in life. He's right though, teenagers do get bored of high school. Mostly cause they aren't doing something interesting. Take me for example. I did horrible my sophomore year, cause I had only one class I really looked forward to, all the others were just a pain to me. Then my junior year and this year, I took classes that interested me, Computer Animation and Web design. I like computers, anything about them interests me. That's why these two year I did better than my sophomore year. So either the education system has to re-think its strategy or teenagers need to get more focused.
